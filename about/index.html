About Us

Nexa Technologies is a leading Solution Provider in Information Technology space especially on IT-Infrastructure / Datacenter, UPS Solutions. Our experienced approach, focus on digital IT, global expertise of solution design, Integrity, project management and technical expertise, have positioned us an emerging IT provider.

Professional services capability sits at the core of our market offer, with a complementary portfolio of services, support, and technology solutions based around Industry Best Practices. Our customers trust not just in our ability to build solutions, but also in the way we design, implement and support these solutions that make their businesses run more efficiently.

Our exceptionally skilled, committed and talented team of consultants, engineers, project managers, and support teams integrate a wide range of technical, business and financial factors when designing the solution for your business.

Our engineering teams have hands-on experience in all aspects of IT solutions, deployment, operational management, and maintenance support. Our world-class architects design solutions that fit best within your environment and help achieve your business goals.

Why Choose Us

At Nexa, we customize our solutions to fit according to your needs, not the other way around. We go out of our way to understand how you work and the challenges you face to have a better understanding of your business. With the latest technology, affordable pricing, experienced technicians and dedicated service are all here to take you to the next level.

We believe in keeping our clients happy, and our history proves so. And our client case stories will support this too!

Nexa offers a full range of IT consulting, services, solutions and support services tailored to achieve results, match your priorities, and improve your organizations productivity.
